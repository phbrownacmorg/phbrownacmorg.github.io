Tags that don't change the look

This section is contained in a <div> tag, indicated by the horizontal lines. The lines are not produced by the tag itself, however, but by its style atttribute.

<span> tags do pretty much the same thing, but at text level rather than block level. One common use of a <span> tag is to format a book title, like so: Magical Creatures I Have Known.

Hyperlinks, which take you somewhere else when you click on them, are what put the Web into World Wide Web, as opposed to the World Wide Collection of Linear Documents. Hyperlinks can also be targets, allowing for linking into the middle of a page.
